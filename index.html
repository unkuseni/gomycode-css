roman's serves spirited, italian-influenced food that reflects a longstanding commitment to local agriculture and sustainable farming. Our menu celebrates our relationships with small scale farmers, tradition-based artisans, agriculture-based abeokuta importers and jos winemakers.

while the experience at roman's showcases our focus on procuring the highest quality produce and wine, we are above all, committed to making your experience and our community as memorable as possible.
